ABOUT ME

My name is Sophie Bergé and I am a nineteen-year-old graphic design student at LUCA School of Arts in Ghent. I’m currently in the second year of my Academic Bachelor’s degree. At present, my main focus is on fashion, architecture and typography. It really is the enormous diversity of graphic design applications and the opportunity to combine multiple modes of creative arts, that make me love this study. It seem to be a limitless source of variation and inspiration and it has already made me look differently at the world.
