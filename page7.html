ABOUT ME

My name is Sophie Bergé and I am a nineteen-year-old graphic design student at LUCA School of Arts in Ghent. I’m currently in the second year of my Academic Bachelor’s degree. My focus is mainly on fashion, architecture and typography. The diversity of graphic design applications and the possibility to combine multiple modes of creative arts is what drew me into it. When having obtained my master’s degree, I will be able to further develop my knowledge and competence in many different directions in the graphic design world thanks to the diverse nature of my education.
