ASSOCIATION

By Sophie Berg√©, Grafisch Ontwerp 2017

My publication explains how feet are washed step by step. I linked this to the Bible story in which Mary Magdalene washed the feet of Jesus before he went to the table. I combined the clean aspect of foot washing with the dirty rumours about Mary Magdalene. It is said that she and Jesus had a relationship and were not that pious at all. In my book I work out this contrast through photographs of feet that are being washed and photographs of ambiguous objects.
