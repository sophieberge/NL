DURA LEX, SED LEX

In this assignment I have shaped the Belgian constitution of 1831, with amendments of 15 November 1920, 24 August and 15 October 1921. I also added a separate annex in which I put one article, namely the article ‘the use of languages spoken in Belgium free’, into the spotlight through a typography that responds to the content of the article.
