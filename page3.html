PARIS MATCH

By Sophie bergé, Graphic Design 2017

Paris is a busy city that always seems to be in motion. That is why I focused on the people who were waiting for something or someone. In my publication I have worked with photos that depict activities occupying people when they are waiting, such as: nail biting, ddling with their hair ... These images I have placed in my book as a kind of video to accentuate the longevity of waiting. the images change faster and faster, making the annoying aspect of waiting clear. The combination with the text ‘Waiting for Godot’ and the page numbering a.d. cigarette butts emphasize the ‘endless’ waiting again. Size (closed): 260 mm x 345 mm
