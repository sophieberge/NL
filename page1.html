HELLVETICA

By Sophie Bergé, Graphice Design 2017
The graphic identity of our group (eight people) is HELLVETICA. Our team conducts a graphic protest and this against ‘graphic perfection’. We break down fonts and people that are normally considered good in the graphic world. We have shown this on signs that are inspired by protest signs.
